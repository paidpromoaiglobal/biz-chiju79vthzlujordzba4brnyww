Our Legacy

MADAN JEWELLERS

Madan Jewellers, located in the heart of Vyasarpadi, Chennai, has been a trusted name for fine jewellery in the local community. Situated at 15C, Muthu Mudali Street, our store offers an exquisite collection of gold, silver, and diamond jewellery designed to suit every occasion — from everyday wear to grand celebrations like weddings and festivals.

We take pride in offering high-quality jewellery at honest prices, serving families across Chennai with transparency and craftsmanship. Whether you are looking for traditional temple jewellery, modern lightweight designs, or custom-made pieces, Madan Jewellers is your destination for beauty, trust, and value. Visit us six days a week and experience the warmth of personalised service that only a neighbourhood jeweller can offer.
